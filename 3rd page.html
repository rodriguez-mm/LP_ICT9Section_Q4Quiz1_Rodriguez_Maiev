CHARACTERS IN NOLI ME TANGERE
Don Rafael Ibarra
  • He is Ibarra's father and was deemed a filibuster by the government, specifically, Father Damaso.
  • He was framed for the murder of a tax collecter and was imprisoned.
Pilosopo Tasyo
  • Pilosopo Tasyo was seen as odd by most of the townsfolk because of the ideas he spoke of.
  • He became close with Ibarra and helped him with his work.
Sisa
  • Sisa is the mother of 2 boys named Basilio and Crispin.
  • She slowly went insane due to the mistreatment from her husband Pedro and goes around town looking for her sons.
